### Sample Rate Parameters

* Sample Data = 16-bit
* Sample Rate (fs) = 48kHz
* Two Audio Channels
* BCLK Rate = 48000 x 16 x 2 = 1.536MHz
* Base Over-sample = 384fs
* XCK Rate = 48000 x 384 = 18.432MHz
